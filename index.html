sigher Apple TV Linking

This site allows you to link your Apple TV to the sigher app for remote app installation.

How to Link Your Apple TV

  1. Go to Apple TV settings
  2. Go to "General"
  3. Go to "Privacy"
  4. Highlight "Share Apple TV analytics" row
  5. Press Play/Pause button on Apple remote
  6. Go to "Add Profile"
  7. Enter the URL provided by the sigher app
  8. Follow instructions on your TV
